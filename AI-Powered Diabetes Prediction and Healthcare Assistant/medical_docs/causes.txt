Several factors can increase the risk of diabetes:

* Family history of diabetes
* Obesity or excess body weight
* Physical inactivity
* High blood pressure
* High cholesterol levels
* Unhealthy diet
* Age over 45 years
* Smoking

Type 1 diabetes is caused by an autoimmune response that attacks insulin-producing cells in the pancreas.

Type 2 diabetes develops when the body becomes resistant to insulin or does not produce enough insulin.
